# 🚀 Portfolio | Abdeljalil Medjadi

Welcome to the source code of my personal portfolio. This project serves as a dynamic digital archive of my journey as a Computer Science Student, capturing both my academic milestones and my self-taught technical explorations.

## 📌 Vision & Purpose
This portfolio isn't just a static resume; it's a living document of my evolution. I built this to:
* Document My Growth: Track my progress through university labs and independent learning.
* Global Opportunities: Present a professional technical identity for international scholarship applications (e.g., Stanford, MEXT).
* Open Exploration: Showcase my "generalist" approach to Computer Science, staying open to all domains of technology.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
To keep the site fast, clean, and responsive, I utilized:
* HTML5 & CSS3: For a structured and minimalist semantic layout.
* Tailwind CSS: For modern, utility-first styling and a "Developer Dark" aesthetic.
* JavaScript: To add interactivity and dynamic elements (like the terminal simulation).
* AI-Assisted Design: Leveraged AI tools to refine the UI/UX and optimize the code architecture.

## 🏆 Featured Section: Projects
The Projects section is the core of this portfolio. It is where theory meets practice. I take pride in showcasing real-world implementations—from low-level Computer Architecture designs (MIPS, Logisim) to Cybersecurity fundamentals—proving that I am a "doer" who builds systems, not just a learner who reads about them.

## 🧠 Philosophy

As a Computer Science student, I believe that coding is a way of thinking and solving problems that transcends the screen. It is about discipline, logic, and the relentless pursuit of understanding how the digital world operates.
